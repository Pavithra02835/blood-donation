Our Service Process

1. Blood Donation Registration

Donors can register themselves through our platform. We collect essential information and notify them when a matching recipient needs their blood group.

2. Request Blood Instantly

Hospitals, patients, or caretakers can submit a blood request form. Our system notifies potential donors in nearby areas based on urgency and availability.

4. 24/7 Emergency Helpline

In life-threatening emergencies, our support team and ambulance network coordinate with blood banks and donors to ensure quick delivery.

5. Post-Donation Follow-up

We ensure that every donor receives post-donation care tips, certificates, and reminders for their next eligibility date.
